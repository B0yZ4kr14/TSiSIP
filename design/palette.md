# TSiSIP Color Palette

## Brand Identity

The TSiSIP palette is anchored in a deep metallic blue that conveys trust, stability, and technical authority. Cold tones dominate to reduce visual fatigue during long NOC shifts.

## Primary Colors

| Name | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|
| Primary Blue | `#1A3A5C` | 26, 58, 92 | Headers, logo, primary actions |
| Primary Blue Light | `#2E5E8C` | 46, 94, 140 | Hover states, focus rings |
| Primary Blue Dark | `#0D1F33` | 13, 31, 51 | Active/pressed states |

## Surface Colors

| Name | Hex | RGB | Usage |

| Surface Card | `#FFFFFF` | 255, 255, 255 | Cards, panels, modals |
